    What Is Solar Boat

    The Solar Ferry Boat is an environmentally-friendly passenger water transportation that does not cause air, sound, or water pollution. The solution combines the advancements in naval architecture, electric power train and photovoltaics to build electric boats and ferries that run on solar energy.
